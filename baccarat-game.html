🎯 WHAT IS BACCARAT?

Baccarat is one of the oldest and most prestigious casino card games in the world, famously favored by James Bond. It's a comparing card game played between two hands: the Player and the Banker. Your job is simply to bet on which hand will be closest to 9.

💡 Key fact: You don't play the hands yourself — you just bet on the outcome. This makes Baccarat one of the easiest casino games to learn!

💡 The magic of 9: If the total exceeds 9, only the last digit counts. So a hand of 7 + 8 = 15 counts as 5. A hand of 9 + 6 = 15 is also 5.

🎮 HOW A ROUND WORKS

  • Step 1 – Place your bet: Choose Banker, Player, or Tie before cards are dealt.
  • Step 2 – Deal: Both Player and Banker receive two cards face up.
  • Step 3 – Natural Win: If either hand totals 8 or 9 with two cards, it's a "Natural" — no more cards are drawn.
  • Step 4 – Third card rule: A third card may be drawn automatically based on strict rules (see below).
  • Step 5 – Closest to 9 wins!

📈 POPULAR BETTING SYSTEMS

Martingale System: Double your bet after each loss. One win recovers all losses. Risky with table limits and big losing streaks.

Paroli System (Reverse Martingale): Double your bet after each win, reset after 3 consecutive wins. Low risk, captures winning streaks.

1-3-2-4 System: Bet 1 unit, then 3, then 2, then 4 in sequence. Reset after a loss or completing the cycle. Limits losses while letting wins compound.

Flat Betting: Bet the same amount every hand. Simple, low variance, best for beginners and bankroll preservation.

⚠️ Important: Betting systems can add structure and fun, but none change the house edge. Always set a loss limit before you play!

💼 BANKROLL MANAGEMENT

  • Set a session budget before you play and never exceed it.
  • A common rule: never bet more than 2-5% of your session bankroll on a single hand.
  • Set a win goal (e.g., +50%) and walk away when you reach it.
  • Avoid chasing losses — streaks of 7-10 losses in a row are statistically normal.
  • Take regular breaks — decision fatigue affects judgment.
