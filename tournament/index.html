TOURNAMENT — PARTICIPANT AGREEMENT & RULES

IMPORTANT NOTICE

This is a non-monetary tournament. No real money is wagered, won, or paid out. All chips are Tournament Units (TUs) with no cash value. Prizes, if any, are non-monetary awards for bragging rights or in-app non-cashable rewards only.

ELIGIBILITY

  1. Participation is limited to invited users who are at least twenty-one (21) years of age.
  2. Employees and/or relatives of the Baccarat Gladiator app developer, its subsidiaries and affiliates, and their advertising agencies or representatives are ineligible.
  3. No monetary fee is required for Tournament entry. Qualification is based on historical play within the Baccarat Gladiator app.

TOURNAMENT FORMAT

  1. Each round consists of fifteen (15) hands.
  2. The first three (3) hands of each round are betting-optional. Participants may choose to wager or abstain.
  3. Participants must place a bet on the fourth (4th) hand and all subsequent hands. Failure to do so results in disqualification.
  4. Participants must make their bets within the app's designated time limit.
  5. Virtual table and seat assignments are determined by random draw. Tournament Management has sole discretion over final assignments.
  6. Participants must play in the round they are qualified for. Participants auto-qualified for a later round may not play in earlier rounds.
  7. Participants do not carry over Tournament chips to the next round. Each round begins with a fresh bankroll of 50,000 TUs.

BETTING RULES

  1. Participants may not wager on BANK and PLAYER simultaneously.
  2. A TIE bet may only be placed in addition to a BANK or PLAYER bet, never as a substitute.
  3. A PAIR bet may only be placed in addition to a BANK or PLAYER bet, never as a substitute.
  4. After a wager is finalized, it cannot be moved or changed.
  5. All bets must be in increments of 100 Tournament Units (TUs).
  6. Commissions will NOT be marked up or collected.

On the 15th hand, the maximum on Bank/Player is removed — Participants may bet up to their full chip count. Tie and Pair maximums always apply.

SECRET BET (HAND 15)

  1. Participants must play one (1) mandatory secret bet on the 15th hand of each round. The secret bet is entered into a private field and is not visible to other Participants until revealed after the hand.
  2. A chip count display occurs following the 14th hand of each round.

ADVANCEMENT & ELIMINATION

  1. Eliminated or disqualified Participants must exit their virtual seat immediately.
  2. In the event of a tie at the end of a round, a final elimination hand is played using secret bets. TIE bets are not permitted in elimination hands.
  3. The number of Participants advancing from each table depends on total entrants, as determined by Tournament Management.

CONDUCT & INTEGRITY

  1. Coaching or unauthorized external assistance is prohibited, including via chat, screen share, or any other means.
  2. English is the official language of the Tournament.
  3. Chips may not be transferred, exchanged, borrowed, loaned, or passed between Participants.
  4. Use of external electronic devices or aids for competitive advantage is prohibited.
  5. Any violation may result in immediate disqualification.

GENERAL PROVISIONS

  1. Disputes must be addressed through the app's dispute mechanism before logging out of the current round. All decisions of the Tournament Committee are final and binding.
  2. Tournament Management reserves the right to cancel, modify, or reschedule the Tournament at any time without prior notice.
  3. Tournament Management shall be excused from performance obligations due to Force Majeure conditions (governmental laws, acts of war, terrorism, acts of nature, pandemic viruses, severe weather, national emergencies, etc.).
  4. Tournament chips (TUs) have no monetary value, may not be used for any other purpose, and expire at the end of each round.
  5. By participating, each Participant agrees to abide by all Tournament Rules. Rules may be changed at any time at Tournament Management's sole discretion.
