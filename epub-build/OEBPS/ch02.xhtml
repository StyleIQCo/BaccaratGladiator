2. EZ Baccarat & Side Bets

EZ Baccarat removes the 5% Banker commission entirely. Instead, a Banker win of exactly 3 cards totaling 7 is a push — no payout, no loss. This single rule replaces the commission with a neutral standoff.

Dragon 7

A side bet available in EZ Baccarat mode. It wins when the Banker wins with a 3-card total of 7.

BetTriggerPayout
Dragon 7Banker 3-card 740:1

Dragon 7 appears roughly once every 27 hands. It also causes the main Banker bet to push rather than lose.

Tiger Bets

Tiger bets are available on Baccarat Gladiator and in select casinos. They target Banker hands totaling 6 with 3 cards.

Standard vs EZ: Most high-limit rooms in Macau and Las Vegas now use EZ Baccarat. Ask the dealer before sitting down.
