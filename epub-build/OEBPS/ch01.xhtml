1. Baccarat Basics

Baccarat is one of the simplest casino games in the world. Two hands are dealt — Player and Banker — and you bet on which will be closer to 9. You are not the Player; you simply bet on an outcome.

Card Values

CardsValueExample
Ace1A♠ = 1
2 through 9Face value7♦ = 7
10, Jack, Queen, King0K♣ = 0
Key rule: Only the last digit counts. A hand of 7 + 8 = 15 scores 5. A hand of 9 + 9 = 18 scores 8.

How a Hand Plays Out

Both Player and Banker start with two cards. A total of 8 or 9 on the first two cards is called a Natural — the hand ends immediately with no more cards drawn.

Otherwise, a third card may be drawn based on fixed rules (see Chapter 3). The hand closest to 9 wins.
